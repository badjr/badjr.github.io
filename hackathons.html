CEO Hacks 2015

  • This was Georgia State University's first hackathon. The theme was "hack the school", where participants come up with hacks to improve the college life.
  • I had an idea for an alternative to being on hold with financial aid for long periods of time, which was a website that allows you to enter a virtual queue, and see your place in the queue as you advance.
  • My role was to implement the website.

Unlock the Box 2015

  • At this civic hackathon, dedicated to expanding voting access and encourage civic participation, our team came up with the idea to make an iOS app and website allowing voters without participation to search for nearby voters volunteering to give rides.
  • My role was to implement the website.
